History of our restaurant

Ernest's House was established in 2012 by Nashon Ernest, a former firefighter and chef. He had long dreamed of opening his own restaurant and set out to make it a reality. Ernest’s House quickly became a beloved landmark in the city, attracting patrons from near and far with its delectable and varied menu offerings.

Ernest’s House celebrates 11 years of service and success, continuing to feed both locals and visiting patrons. Their recipe for success: providing customers with quality food, great service, and a whole lot of heart.
